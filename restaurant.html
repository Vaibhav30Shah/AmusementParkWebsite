VILLA BISTARO

Contemporary bistro and lounging space


Midtown's latest offering, the Villa Bistro stores a unique dining and lounging experience for you. Eloquent design aesthetics, the restaurant offers a unique blend of comfort and luxury. You can choose from a plethora of handcrafted cocktails, premier cigars, and a range of exceptional single malts. The restaurant seats 44, and is a utopia for a special gathering with your most important guests.




CAFE 24

24 HOUR FINE DINING MULTI-CUISINE RESTAURANT


Café 24 brings you the treasures from the world cuisine, round-the-clock with an eclectic fine dining experience. The magnificent Café 24 seats 200 people and serves from a scrumptious breakfast spread to varied multi-cuisine choices for lunch and dinner making it the best restaurant in Lonavala. For the night owls we have plenty of options for you to satiate your palette way past everyone's bedtime.




PNF RESTAURANT

INDIAN, MALVANI & ORIENTAL CUISINE


PNF Restaurant and Bar, an exotic multi cuisine restaurant featuring the best of Indian speciality which offers a blend of culinary innovations with traditional methods. If you are someone who just cant get over Oriental cuisine, then you will be delighted by the piping hot dim sums and succulent sushi served here.




PARSI DHABA

AUTHENTIC PARSI & PUNJABI DHABA CUISINES


Parsi Dhaba serves authentic delicacies from traditional aromatic gravies and spiced rice to rich flavours. While you are here do not miss the old favorites such as Dhansak, Salli Boti and kopra pak. This restaurant gives a new meaning to the dhaba in lonavala offering some mouth watering and lip smacking Punjabi food in an enriching dining experience with the view of the adventure park from colossal glass paneled windows overlooking the restaurant lawn.
