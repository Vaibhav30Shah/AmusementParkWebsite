A HAPPY BIRTHDAY LIKE NEVER BEFORE

You shouldn’t need an excuse to visit Midtown Adventure and Resorts however a birthday is as good a time and occasion to spend with us as any. Just for the record, we throw the most amazing birthday parties here at Della. Allow us to host your birthday party the Della Way. We truly are a one stop destination for adventurers and thrill seekers of all ages and we know just how you’d like to celebrate your special day!

And not just you, we assure you that your family and friends will be talking about this adventurously unique birthday party for many, many years to come. After all that’s what a birthday party is for, to make the most awesome memories.

As a Birthday gift we offer you a free pass, on your special day. And we love that you are here celebrating with your family and friends so we offer a Flat 15% discount to them as well. With over 50+ hair raising, adrenaline pumping activities for you to indulge in, we guarantee you a birthday party like you’ve never had before.

IF IT’S A BIRTHDAY PARTY…THERE HAS TO BE BIRTHDAY CAKE Midtown is always prepared to add that extra bit of value to your special day. Our pastry chefs whip up the most delicious and exquisite bespoke cakes for any and all occasions. You can also indulge the sweeter part of your palette with delicacies and goodies from our bakery and the Della Fudge and Chikki Store. Gorge on signature specialties which include the liquor laced fudges, assorted homemade chikkis amongst so much more. PLAY HARD PARTY HARDER AT P18.

Della Adventure and Resorts proudly boasts of P18, one of the hottest and most sizzling night clubs in Lonavala and you could not be in a better place to bring in that birthday party. Suave, hip and happening, P18 belts out the biggest chart busters one after another as you enjoy a delightful menu, delicious cocktails and even a great outdoor lounge. We are also happily confident of suggesting and arranging themed décor, a bonfire and elegant sheeshas to earmark a truly memorable memory for you and your family and friends!
